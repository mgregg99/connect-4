Connect 4

Rules

The goal of the game is to connect four of your pieces in a row. The pieces are represented by red and yellow circles. The game is played on a 7x6 grid. The game is played with two players. The first player to connect four of their pieces in a row wins.

How to use this site

This site uses a clinet to play the game. Follow this link to see the source code and download the needed files. Enjoy!
